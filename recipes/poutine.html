Real Poutine

Description

Poutine is an indulgence of crispy French fries, savory gravy, and melted cheese curds.

Make this delicious Canadian specialty at home with this easy recipe!

Ingredients

  • Oil: You'll need a quart of vegetable oil for frying.
  • Gravy: Use a store-bought can of beef gravy or make your own at home.
  • Fries: Russet potatoes work well for French fries, as they're extra starchy.
  • Cheese curds: Cheese curds are key for authentic poutine. However, if you like, you can use shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Heat the oil to 365 degrees F.
  2. Warm the gravy on the stove or in the microwave.
  3. Fry the French fries and drain on paper towels.
  4. Place the fries on a serving platter, then top with curds and warm gravy.
